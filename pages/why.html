Why be a Computer Scientist?

Computer Science is a field that has grown and will continue to grow. A career in Computer Science is fulfilling, as you can make software that helps people.

A Computer Science career is also secure. With the abundance of jobs available, it will not be difficult for you to find work and gain income.

Computer Science jobs pay well, often starting around $60,000 a year. Breaking the $100,000 mark is not uncommon. To learn more about salaries in your local area, check out PayScale for more information!
